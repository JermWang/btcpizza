About

Plain-language answers to the questions every holder asks first.

·What is Jensen Strategy?

An onchain reward system that routes project creator fees into NVDAx and distributes to holders via epoch-based snapshots.

·What is NVDAx?

NVDAx is NVIDIA xStock — a tokenized NVIDIA exposure asset on Solana. It does not grant equity, voting, dividends, or direct ownership of NVIDIA common stock.

·How are rewards calculated?

Reward share = wallet balance ÷ total eligible balance at the epoch's snapshot. Same formula for every wallet.

·Why do epochs get longer?

Early epochs prove the system fast. Later epochs space out so reward pools can compound into larger NVDAx batches.

·How do I verify rewards?

Each epoch publishes holder snapshot data, a manifest hash, distribution batches, and Solscan-verifiable transfer receipts.

·Compliance

Jensen Strategy is a parody/community project. Not affiliated with or endorsed by NVIDIA Corporation. NVDAx is a tokenized representation and does not grant equity ownership.
