How to climb

  • 1
    Hold longer. Time weight keeps compounding while the wallet stays in.
  • 2
    Hold more. Balance weight lifts your score for the next snapshot.
  • 3
    Stay ready. WBTC ATA readiness keeps eligible wallets from getting skipped.

Fairness Layer

  • ✓
    Same formula for everyone. Score = holding weight + holding-time multiplier.
  • ✓
    Public snapshot. Round manifests prove the eligible wallet set once the holder indexer is configured.
  • ✓
    Onchain outcome. Final WBTC transfers become clickable Solscan receipts when real batch signatures exist.

Round Rules

  • •
    Top scored wallets make the next WBTC drop until the round cap is filled.
  • •
    Payout size follows score share, so higher rank means a larger slice.
  • •
    If you miss this round, your wallet can climb before the next one.
