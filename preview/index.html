How to get a bigger slice

  • 1
    Hold more. Reward share is wallet balance / total eligible snapshot balance.
  • 2
    Make the cut. Wallets inside the current holder cap at snapshot time are included first.
  • 3
    Stay ready. WBTC ATA readiness keeps eligible wallets from getting skipped.

Fairness Layer

  • ✓
    Same formula for everyone. Reward share = wallet balance / total eligible snapshot balance.
  • ✓
    Public snapshot. Round manifests prove the eligible wallet set once the holder indexer is configured.
  • ✓
    Onchain outcome. Final WBTC transfers become clickable Solscan receipts when real batch signatures exist.

Round Rules

  • •
    Top balance wallets make the next WBTC drop until the round cap is filled.
  • •
    Payout size follows token balance share, so a bigger snapshot balance means a larger slice.
  • •
    If you miss this round, your wallet can qualify before the next snapshot.
